### Content v1 — General Audience

**Incubation Project — Securing the Future of small / medium sized enterprises**

This is a brief introduction to a project we are working on in the Vali Entrepreneurship Hub. We are sending it to people whose perspective we want to learn from, and to companies whose situation may resemble what we describe.

**Where the Idea Originated.** I worked in my family's business in steel manufacturing for over two years learning how it operates. My assignment was to digitalize the analogue processes that ran on intuition and experience. I understood the weight of the knowledge trapped within senior employees' minds and the implications of formalizing it.

On the other hand, Giuseppe coming from the banking world, deeply rooted in the Italian SMEs ecosystem, seeing the same businesses from the outside, where financing or acquiring these companies meant confronting the same opacity, businesses that clearly worked, but whose inner logic lived in people rather than in documents. Two different vantage points, the same structural gap.

**What we believe.** Documenting Operations in organically grown SMEs will free up mental capacity in founders and senior employees to focus on how to secure the company's future rather than running daily operations. Being the provider of such an organizational map is a foot in the door to accompany exactly those future-securing processes it enables such as generational hand-over, digitalization, merger or acquisition.

We believe this is now solvable in a way it wasn't five years ago: recent developments in AI mean that the tacit knowledge inside a business can be observed, mapped, and turned into something the rest of the company can actually use efficiently enough to significantly outperform consulting boutiques that would traditionally have performed this task.

**How we want to do it.** We go to the company location and work through interviews, document review, computer-use tracking, and direct shadowing of how they actually work to understand operations and decision making. With an iterative process of inputting the collected information into our ai enabled tool that maps, organizes and documents everything we cycle around and crystalize a concrete output: a report / map of how the business runs (which in and of itself is the springboard of how to act on it).

**What it enables**
- Smoother generational handover, from founder to successor or from one expert generation to the next
- Freed-up capacity in the founder's head, less of it spent on running the business as it is, more of it on innovation and business development
- A real foundation for digitalization, in businesses that have postponed it for years
- A more readable company for M&A, clearer processes mean clearer due diligence

*Wolf & Giuseppe*

> **What changed from v1:** Rewritten in direct second-person voice; four conviction bullet points introduced (Tech Fatigue, Clean Room Rule, Consultant Tax, Founder Freedom); "Operations Blueprint" named as the output; origin story restructured into "Who We Are" + "Our Foundation"

**Understanding the real problems of small / medium enterprises**

**Who We Are.** My name is Wolf, and I am currently a Master's student at ESMT Berlin. Together with my classmate Giuseppe, who brings a deep background from the banking side of the Italian SME ecosystem, we are utilizing our university's startup incubation program to understand and hopefully contribute to solving a specific, painful reality we believe traditional businesses have.

**Our Foundation (Where this comes from).** Before coming to Berlin, I spent over two years working directly on the factory floor of my family's steel manufacturing business, FILIGRAN, in Poland. My task was practically impossible: taking deeply ingrained, analog processes that ran purely on the intuition of veteran employees, and trying to force them into a structured system.

I was ruthlessly confronted with how hard it actually is to standardize flexible workflows and how much of a pain in the ass modernization of something that already works well can be. From human resistance to compromising analogue flexibility for the efficiency of standardization. Not to mention the inefficiency that change inevitably carries with it.

**What We Believe.** We are building this project based on a few convictions:
- **The Tech Fatigue:** Buzzwords like digitalization, AI solution, efficiency piss people off. Its kind of like consultant bullshit who come with fluffy theories and platforms that ignore the reality of a busy, organically grown company.
- **The "Clean Room" Rule:** We believe you cannot simply modernize or transition a business without first mapping its messy, analog reality. You have to untangle the knots before you build the future.
- **The Consultant Tax is Broken:** We believe that traditional management boutiques are too slow, unnecessarily disruptive, and too expensive for what they actually deliver.
- **Founder Freedom:** We believe that documenting your operations is the only way to free up your mental capacity, allowing you to stop running daily operations and finally focus on securing the company's future.

**How we want to do it.** We absorb the technological headache for you. We come to your location and act as a neutral mirror. We shadow workflows, review documents, and have conversations with your team. Behind the scenes, we use advanced, AI-enabled analytical tools to process all of this organic information. Your team doesn't learn a single piece of new software. We use the tools on our end; you just get the finalized result.

**The Value We Deliver.** Within weeks, we crystallize your company's intuition into a concrete output: a finalized, highly structured Operations Blueprint.

This is a tangible asset that protects your legacy. It provides a real foundation for the future, ensuring a smoother generational handover from founder to successor. If your goal is a sale, it makes your company instantly readable to external buyers and banks, protecting your valuation during due diligence.

Most importantly, it untethers you from the daily fires. It gives you the freedom to step back with the confidence that your life's work is secure, documented, and fully prepared for whatever comes next.

We are currently speaking with experienced founders to understand the specific operational "knots" you face. We would welcome a brief conversation to learn from your perspective.

*Wolf & Giuseppe*
