## Channels Ranked by Effectiveness

### 1. Commercialisti (accountants) ⭐ Highest EV
The most trusted figure in an Italian SME founder's life. 2-3 commercialisti who like what you're doing can introduce you to 15-20 clients.
- **Action:** 5-10 emails this week to commercialisti specialized in PMI in target geography
- **Template:** [[Email Templates#Commercialista version]]

### 2. Warm intros through accelerator network
Mentors, sponsors, partner firms, professors. One intro from someone the founder respects beats 50 cold emails.
- **Action:** Map the Vali network for SME connections

### 3. Industry associations and family-business networks
- Confindustria local chapters
- AIdAF (Associazione Italiana delle Aziende Familiari)
- University family-business programs (Bocconi SDA, Cattaneo LIUC)
- German Mittelstand associations (BVMW)

### 4. Second-generation successors on LinkedIn
The 30-40 year old son/daughter already in the business. Responsive, on LinkedIn, often frustrated with parent's approach. Proxy source + can open door to parent.

### 5. Showing up in person
Trade fairs in specific verticals. Italian business is relational. A coffee in Brescia is worth ten emails.

### 6. Cold email to founders (lowest yield)
Response rate: under 1% for succession topics through generic addresses. Supplement only, not primary channel.
- **Template:** [[Email Templates#Cold founder version]]

## Tactical Notes

- **Find real names** via Camera di Commercio / Registro Imprese — never send to `info@`
- **Send from a real person** — personal name, personal phone number
- **Follow up twice then stop** — Day 0, Day 7, Day 21
- **Realistic math:** 150 cold emails = 3-8 replies = 2-5 conversations. 10 commercialista emails = 2 say yes = 15+ founder intros.
- **Offer something back** — anonymized benchmark report of findings
